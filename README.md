# Laterly — Write to your future self

Laterly is a simple web app that lets you write a letter to your future self and read it later.

🌐 **Live website:** https://laterly.xyz

---

## ✨ What is Laterly?

Have you ever wanted to send a message to your future self?

Laterly helps you:

* write a letter today
* lock it until a future date
* come back and read it later

It's a simple and emotional way to capture your thoughts over time.

## 💡 Why Laterly?

Some things only make sense later.
